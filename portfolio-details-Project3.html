Project information

  • Category: Web Application

Blinkay is an app-based platform for booking parking spaces. Users can search, find and book the parking space near to their location by providing details like type of parking space, time, personal details, etc. It offers features like pay and claims the parking fare, invoice generation, and more. The mobile app is available for both Android and iOS devices.

Blinkay App

Pay the parking meter from your mobile. Use our app and forget about line-ups and missing coins.

Blinkay Appeal

An appeal is the process in which cases are reviewed by a higher authority, where parties request a formal change to an official decision. Appeals function both as a process for error correction as well as a process of clarifying and interpreting the law.

The concept of appeal requires the existence of a judicial hierarchy. We don’t have the hierarchy system here from lower courts to supreme courts appeal processing but we have relation from the customer’s ticket to clerk to legal framework which can take the decisions like reduction of fine with due date, confirmation of offence, cancellation etc for screening and hearings the fines of requested ticket.
